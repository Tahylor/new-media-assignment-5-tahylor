Welcome!

Welcome to K Krystals! We are two sisters that want our customers to feel beautiful with our skincare and jewelry products. We make all of our merchandise by hand and with love.

Meet the CEOs

Krystin Waston

Hello! My name is Krystin Watson, and I create the jewelry for K Krystals. I am a young African American teenager trying to showcase black girl magic through jewelry. All jewelry is made by hand with just string and beads. By creating jewelry, I hope to help people express themselves by using beads and string.
